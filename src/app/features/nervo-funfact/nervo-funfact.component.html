Fun Facts


Did you know that neurons are the longest-living cells in the human body? They are irreplaceable, so don’t lose any.


The neurons have giant appetites, so they need a steady supply of glucose and oxygen. 25 percent of the calories you take in every day is used to supply your brain’s activity!


The longest nerve in the body is the sciatic nerve. It runs from the spinal cord to the toes on each side of the body.


The brain uses more of the body’s energy than the rest of the organs, using over 20% of the energy reserves in the body.
